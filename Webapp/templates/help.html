Help/FAQ

  1. How do I use this site?
    Answer: There is a How it Works page on the homepage and sidebar.

  2. I filled out a servey but no restaurant showed up when I searched.
    Answer: This can be caused by a few things. To fix this try adjusting the range of the search and adding more restaurant types.

  3. Do I need an account to use this site?
    Answer: No you can click the get started button on the homepage when you're not logged in and fill out the Dining Preferences to search.

  4. How do I add my friends?
    Answer: Go to the Connect page and select the search for friends tab and type in the email for the person you would like to friend.

  5. How do I create a Group?
    Answer: Go to the Connect page and select the Groups Page type in a group name and click the plus icon.

  6. I think I bug how do I let the developers know?
    Answer: On the contact us page there is information to get in contact with us.

  7. What are the terms and policies for this website?
    Answer: When you go to create an account you can click the Terms & Privacy link.

  8. I forgot my password what do I do?
    Answer: Go to the login page and type in your email and select forgot password.
